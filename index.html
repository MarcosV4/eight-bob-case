Exercise 2 · The approach

Secure the base
to finance the offensive.

  • DensifyThe healthy core — Florida, California, Texas, Illinois. Replicate proven, diversified density.
  • CaptureThe rockets — New York, Georgia. Lean into explosive growth, channelled through select niche retailers.
  • DeepenExisting accounts first. Several priority states already have one of their leading niche retailers as a client. Reactivate dormant accounts before chasing new doors.

All three run in parallel, from now — calibrated by tiers (how much each market deserves) and phases (how fast to scale, as evidence accumulates).

In closing

One coherent case.

I

Protect the core

Sixteen products carry the business through the peak. Position stock in advance; decide the big order on real data.

II

Separate the channels

Web is a national margin engine, not a state. Geography and channel each get the strategy they deserve.

III

Scale on evidence

Capture exceptional growth where the data supports it — calibrated by tier and phase, financed by a secured base.

Capture the boom — without betting blind.
